About Us

Our History

Founded in 1967, Georgetown University Rugby Football Club is a club sport team that has competed at the highest levels of collegiate rugby since its inception. The team competes in the Mid-Atlantic Rugby Conference in Division II of National Collegiate Rugby. Our team carries the values of our institution onto the rugby field, and vice versa. The team culture that has been passed on from class to class continues because of the active involvement of our alumni network. They play an active role supporting the next generation of Hoyas in their rugby and professional careers.

Our Commitment to Inclusivity

Inclusivity is at the heart of everything we do. Rugby is for everyone, regardless of experience or background. Our club is a welcoming space where individuals from diverse walks of life come together, united by their love for the game. We embrace and celebrate the richness of different perspectives and identities in our community. Whether you're a seasoned player or stepping onto the field for the first time, GURFC is your home.

Developing Athletes, Building Leaders

GURFC is more than a sports team; it's a platform for personal growth and leadership development. Through our rigorous training sessions, our coaching staff not only hones players' rugby skills but also instills values like discipline, resilience, and teamwork. We encourage our members to take on leadership roles on and off the field, fostering a culture of accountability and empowerment. The lessons learned on the pitch translate into life skills that extend far beyond the game.

Beyond Rugby: A Community of Support

Joining GURFC means gaining a support network that extends beyond the game itself. Our members are dedicated to supporting one another academically, physically, and personally. From study groups and fitness sessions to being there for each other through life's challenges, GURFC fosters an environment where teammates become lifelong friends. Our community is one where bonds forged on the field translate into lasting relationships and a sense of belonging that lasts well beyond college.

Embracing Tradition

As a part of GURFC, you're not just joining a team but becoming part of a rich tradition. We take pride in our history, honoring the legacy of those who have worn our jersey before us. From game-day rituals to alumni events, we cherish the traditions that define our club. Moreover, we're committed to creating new memories and experiences for our members, ensuring that every season brings moments that will be cherished for a lifetime.
